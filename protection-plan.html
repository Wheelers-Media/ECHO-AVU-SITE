Frequently Asked Questions

When does my coverage start?

Coverage begins immediately upon purchase for 5 years. For the first year, it complements the manufacturer's warranty.

Is the plan transferrable?

Yes. If you sell your equipment, the remaining coverage transfers to the new owner, adding resale value.

What if my product can't be fixed?

If a repair isn't possible or cost-effective, we will provide a credit for the original purchase price toward a replacement.

Are commercial applications covered?

This specific plan involves residential use only. For commercial or industrial integration coverage, please contact our support team for a bespoke maintenance agreement.
