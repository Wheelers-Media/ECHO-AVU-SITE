The marine environment is harsh. UV rays, saltwater, and humidity destroy standard car audio gear in months. We install purpose-built marine systems designed to last.

Will This Drain My Battery?
We design systems with dual-battery isolation switches. This allows you to play music while anchored without worrying about your engine starting battery dying.
What Is The Difference Between Car and Marine Audio?
Marine gear is conformal coated to protect circuit boards from corrosion and uses UV-resistant materials that won't crack or fade in the sun.
Tower Speakers vs. Cabin Speakers
Tower speakers project sound 50+ feet behind the boat for wakeboarders, while cabin speakers fill the cockpit. A good system balances both.

Roof Mount vs. Dash Mount?
Roof systems stay cleaner and project sound better at speed, while dash/kick-panel systems provide better mid-bass response. We often combine both.
