For nearly 20 years, Echo AVU has served as the Bronze Sponsor and exclusive technology partner for the Rotary Dream Home Lottery.

Our Philosophy

More Than Just a Show Home.

The Dream Home is our "Living Showroom." It allows us to demonstrate how technology integrates into architectural design.

From the early days of dedicated "Man Cave" theaters to today's invisible whole-home automation and golf simulators, the Dream Home proves that technology isn't just seen. It's experienced.

20 Years of Partnership

Decades of Innovation.

The Cinema Era

2006-2015

Bringing the movie theater home with dedicated projection rooms and 7.1 surround sound.

The Connected Home

2016-2020

Moving technology out of the basement. Multi-room audio, outdoor entertainment decks, and the rise of the "Smart Home".

The Invisible Era

Today

Technology that disappears. Hidden architectural speakers, automated shading, Golf Simulators, and voice-controlled lighting scenes.
